I would like to welcome all of the family, friends, staff, and faculty that are here to support our graduates. Today is an incredibly exciting day, and we should take a moment to reflect on our journey. Some of us came straight from high school, some transferred here from community colleges and others have been here for more than four years. The bottom line is that we have all completed our degrees. No matter how long we have been at SSP, and what struggles we have gone through to earn our diplomas, we should be proud of ourselves. We’ve finally made it!

Five years ago, I moved here to attend college. I was just a boy from Comoros with some family that lived in Comoros. I didn’t know anyone at the Faculty of Engineering (SSP), nor did I understand what computer science and engineering really involved. Well now we know, or at least our professors hope we do.
